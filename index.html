Why Join MPMC Practical IT Training

Meraki Printing and Media College, having recognized a direct correlation between unemployment and employable skills, deemed it necessary to equip as many people as possible with skills to help bridge the unemployment gap. Founded on the principles of excellence, we have enlisted qualified teaching and non-teaching staff to carry out this mandate
